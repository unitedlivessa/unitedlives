About United Lives

The live streaming ecosystem is fragmented. Creators and organizations must go live separately on multiple platforms, monitor chats in different places, and track analytics across disconnected dashboards.

United Lives solves this by providing one centralized interface: connect platforms once (OAuth), then control streaming, visibility, and performance monitoring from one place — From One To Many.
